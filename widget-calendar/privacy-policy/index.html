Privacy Policy

Widget Calendar

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Widget Calendar ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information.

Widget Calendar does not require login or account creation. You can use all features of the app without providing any personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide: We do not collect any personal information directly from you, as our app does not require login or registration.

Automatically Collected Information: We use Firebase Analytics to collect certain diagnostic and usage data to improve our app:

  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous device IDs for analytics purposes
  • Crash Data: Information about app crashes to help us fix bugs
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics to optimize user experience
  • Usage Diagnostics: General usage patterns to understand how features are used

This data is not linked to your identity and is used solely to improve app functionality and stability.

3. Calendar Access

Widget Calendar uses Apple's EventKit framework to access your device's calendar. This allows you to:

  • View your calendar events in the widget
  • Create and manage events directly from the app

Your calendar data is processed entirely on your device. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your calendar events on our servers.

4. Data Storage

Local Storage: App settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

iCloud Backup: If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your anniversary data may be backed up to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is not accessible to us.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): For anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting. You can learn more about how Google handles data at Firebase Privacy Policy.

6. Push Notifications

Widget Calendar may send push notifications to remind you of upcoming anniversaries and events. This feature is optional and can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Widget Calendar is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
